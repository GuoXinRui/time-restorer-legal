# Privacy Policy for Time Restorer

**Last Updated: May 2026**

Welcome to Time Restorer. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect
### a. User-Provided Content
- **Images**: When you upload a photo for restoration, we process the image to provide the AI restoration service. We do not store your images permanently on our servers unless required for processing; images are typically deleted after the task is completed.

### b. Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: We may collect basic device information (OS version, device model) to optimize app performance.
- **Advertising ID**: For users on the free tier, we may collect advertising IDs (IDFA/AAID) to deliver personalized ads via our ad partners.

### c. Payment Information
- **Subscription Data**: We use third-party payment processors (Apple App Store and Google Play Store via RevenueCat). We do not store your credit card or banking details on our servers.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- **Service Delivery**: To perform AI colorization and face enhancement on your photos.
- **Monetization**: To verify your Pro subscription status and display ads to free users.
- **Analytics**: To track app usage and improve the restoration experience via anonymous events.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We share data with the following essential providers:
- **AI Providers**: Images are sent to our AI processing partners (e.g., Replicate, ModelScope) for restoration.
- **Payment Processors**: RevenueCat is used to manage subscriptions.
- **Ad Networks**: AdMob or AppLovin may collect device IDs for ad delivery.
